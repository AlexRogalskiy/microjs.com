Fantastic Micro-Frameworks and
Micro-Libraries for Fun and Profit!
For many projects, classical monolithic JavaScript frameworks like jQuery, Prototype.js, Dojo or YUI are just too much. While they of course bring a wealth of features, they come at a price: features you don't need and large file sizes and long download times—especially annoying for mobile sites and apps.

Micro-Frameworks to the rescue! Small, precise, to-the-point code that does one thing only, and does it well, without being encumbered by years of cruft, featuritis, and cross-browser madness.

This sites helps you discover what is available and pick and browse various framework and libraries. Want to add your own? Fork on GitHub, add it to data.js and provide a pull request.
